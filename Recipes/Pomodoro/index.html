How to make Spaghetti alla carbonara

Recipe impovised by Ondřej Honus
  1. One portion of spaghetti (or any other pasta, idc)
  2. Like 12 small ahh tomatoes
  3. Like 50ml of oil
  4. One smaller onion
  5. A good amount of parmesan cheese
  6. Salt
  7. Pepper
  8. Paprika powder

PS: I ain't here to give you exact amounts, do I look like your grandma?

I kinda came up with this one also.

Instructions:

Start making spaghetti, I'm sure you know how. Put a generous amount of oil into a bigger size pan and heat it up.

Cut your onion into small cubes. When the oil is hot, put your onion pieces into the pan and stir often. Start mashing your garlic piece.

After 1-2 minutes of frying the onion pieces put the garlic in the pan and stir it thoroughly.

Now is the time to put the small tomatoes in your pan. Put a lid on the pan, lower the heat a little bit, now you wait for like 4 minutes,

but you probably want to stir it a little to not burn the onion. When you see that the tomatoes are starting to crack, take a masher and mash the tomatoes.

Now it's starting to look like sauce, but you probably want to add some spices. I usually add black pepper, salt, red paprika powder and oregano.

Now just stir the sauce until the tomato water kinda evaporates. Now when the sauce looks ready, put your spaghetti (or really any kind of pasta)

Into the pan and stir it for a minute or two. Now you can serve it into a plate and enjoy your meal!
